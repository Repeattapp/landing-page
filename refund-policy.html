1. Eligibility for Refunds

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

2. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact Us: Send an email to help@repeattapp.com.

  • Submit Within Time Frame: Ensure that the request is submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.

3. Time Frame

  • Processing Time: Refund requests are processed within 10-15 business days of approval.
  • Submission Window: Requests must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date to be eligible.

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Notification: You will be notified via email once the refund has been processed.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Monthly and Weekly Subscriptions: After the first 30 days, monthly and weekly subscriptions are non-refundable.
  • Promotional Items: Items or services received for free as part of a promotion are non-refundable.

6. Weekly Subscription Policy

  • Trial Period: If you subscribed to a weekly plan with a free trial period, you must cancel before the trial period ends to avoid charges.
  • Cancellation: Weekly subscriptions can be canceled at any time, but refunds are not available after the trial period or for partially used weeks.

7. Contact Information

For refund requests or any inquiries related to refunds, please contact us at:

  • Email: help@repeattapp.com
  • Customer Support: Available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM (GMT+1).

8. Exceptions

  • Denial of Requests: We reserve the right to deny refund requests that do not meet our eligibility criteria or are submitted after the specified time frame.
  • Abuse of Policy: Refund requests deemed to be abusive or fraudulent will be denied.

9. Additional Information

  • Pro-Rated Refunds: For annual subscriptions, refunds are calculated based on the remaining months of the subscription.
  • Dispute Resolution: In case of disputes regarding refunds, please contact our support team for resolution.
