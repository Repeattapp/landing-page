Why does it matter?

Did you know that nearly 37% of Americans recently rated their mental health as fair or poor? Additionally, around 66% of Americans are anxious about their health, and 65% worry about paying bills or expenses. Stress, anxiety, and depression can lead to physical symptoms if not addressed.

Positive thinking has been shown to significantly benefit mental health, particularly in managing ADHD. Studies suggest fostering a positive mindset can improve focus, reduce symptoms, and enhance overall well-being.

Repeatt is here to help you overcome ADHD through positive thinking, providing the tools and strategies to foster a healthier mindset.
